About

Abhinandan 2K23

The Abhinandan Induction at SVVV offers a thorough introduction to college life, equipping new students with essential skills, insights, and support. The program covers campus navigation, academic understanding, support services, university policies, and student engagement. It emphasizes peer connections, effective study techniques, cultural diversity appreciation, and technological proficiency. This induction ensures students are well-prepared for their academic journey, fostering a sense of belonging and offering tools for success.
